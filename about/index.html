About Me

I received my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering, with specialization in Software, and my M.Sc. degree in Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on Medical Data Mining. My thesis was on reducing unnecessary laboratory blood tests in ICU GI bleeding patients. My research was done on the MIMIC-II clinical data.

I have the privilege to continue my studies as a PhD student at the University of Nottingham in my field of interest under the supervision of Dr. Xin Chen and Dr. Andrew French. I am also working on Mass Spectrometry Imaging under the supervision of Dr. Iain Styles at the University of Birmingham.

My Research Interests Include:

  • Data Mining
  • Machine Learning
  • Health and Biomedical Informatics
  • Computational Biology
